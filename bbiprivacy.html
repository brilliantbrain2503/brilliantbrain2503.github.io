Privacy Policy

We may collect certain information when you use our app, including:

How We Use Information

We may use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

Our app may display third-party advertisements. These advertisers may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your device and usage patterns for targeted advertising purposes.
